SMIL: Synchronized Multimedia Integration Language

SMIL is a language designed to simplify the creation of multimedia presentations. Unlike HTML and XML, SMIL enables the integration of media such as text, images, and audio in a synchronized manner.

Advantages of SMIL over XML and HTML:

  • Specifically designed for multimedia synchronization.
  • Reduces complexity when handling time-based media.
  • Provides built-in support for multimedia layouts, sequencing, and transitions.
  • Ideal for applications like presentations and digital signage.
